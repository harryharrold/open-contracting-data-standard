There are many different ways in which information on public contracts might be useful. User stories are simply ways of describing these. We are inviting you to share your user story. How do you, or groups and individuals you know, want to use public contracting information and data?

Start by completing the sentence below...

As a I want to
so that

The details you provide here will be used to identify the priorities for the early drafts of the Open Contracting Data Standard, and will be used to construct detailed requirements.
